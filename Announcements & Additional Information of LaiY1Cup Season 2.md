
# LaiY1Cup Season 2

-   Open Registration Period: January 25, 2026, 00:00 to June 5, 2026, 24:00 (Beijing Time)

-   Open Qualifier Dates: July 5-9, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Group Play Dates: July 10-15, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   Playoffs Dates: July 19-21, 2026 (Beijing Time)

-   The Grand Final Date: July 24, 2026 (beijing Time)

-   Tournament Operator:LYC Team

-   Tournament Tier:Tier 2。

## Team Registration

-   This tournament doesn't include Direct VRS invite.All the teams will be invited by Open Qualifiers.
-   This tournament only open to the Chinese teams.
-   The registration is free, Teams will not pay any fee.
-   The way to register is supported by 5e platform.

## Open Qualifiers
-   This stage will be held online.
-   The game server will be an online server located in China.
-   format: Single-elimination
    - All matches are BO1.
    - The top 16 teams will advance to Group play

## Group Play
-   This stage will be held online.
-   The game server will be an online server located in China.
-   format: Swizz Group
    - 0-0/0-1/1-0/1-1 BO1
    - 2-0/0-2/2-1/1-2/2-2 BO3
    - The top 8 teams will advance to Playoffs.

## Playoffs
-   This stage will be held online.
-   The game server will be an online server located in China.
-   format: Single-elimination
    - All matches are BO3.
    - The top 2 teams will advance to The Grand Final.

## The Grand Final
-   This stage will be held online.
-   The game server will be an online server located in China.
-   format: BO5

## Prize Pool: 145 USD
-   Champions: 145 USD
